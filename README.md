# Nishkriti AI

**Nishkriti AI** is a voice-first AI medical operating system for functional medicine clinics in India. It transforms how doctors handle patient consultations by providing a real-time AI copilot during calls, and an automated post-call clinical pipeline that structures data, generates protocols, and builds patient plans — all while keeping the doctor as the final decision-maker.

## The Problem

Functional medicine practitioners face a severe bottleneck: each new patient consultation requires 45–90 minutes of structured data collection before the doctor can even begin clinical reasoning. This limits doctor capacity, causes burnout, and leads to unstructured data and patient follow-up failure.

## The Solution

Nishkriti AI introduces a new operating model:

- **During the Call:** The doctor handles every patient call. An AI assistant — visible only to the doctor — listens to the live transcript and continuously generates intelligent follow-up questions to ensure completeness.
- **After the Call:** A 6-agent AI pipeline automatically structures the data, generates clinical hypotheses, detects red flags, and builds investigation/treatment plans.
- **Doctor Review:** The doctor makes and approves the plans at specific checkpoints.
- **Patient Delivery:** The system handles all delivery and follow-up automatically via WhatsApp, requiring zero app downloads from the patient.

The result is that doctors spend their time on clinical judgment instead of data collection, effectively multiplying their patient capacity while maintaining clinical quality.
